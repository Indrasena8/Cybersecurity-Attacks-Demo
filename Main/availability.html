Availability

Availability is the property that information is accessible and modifiable in a timely fashion by those authorized to do so.

Availability related problems: 403 error, 404 error (may be seen on sites like Infinite Campus), etc.

Methods to Ensure Availability

Denial of Service (DOS)

Denial of Service (DOS): An attempt to overload a website or network to degrade its performance or render it inaccessible.

Physical Protection

Infrastructure is meant to keep information available even during physical challenges.

Ex: Signal barriers to prevent attempts of interference.

Computational Redundancies

Computers and storage devices that serve as fallbacks in the case of failures.

Ex: Backup files, backup stored in USBs, backup computer devices to access when a problem occurs, etc.
